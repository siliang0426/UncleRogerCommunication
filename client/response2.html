Doctor, I've been experiencing frequent heartburn lately. What could be causing it, and how can I relieve the discomfort?

Me

I'm sorry to hear about your frequent heartburn. There are several potential causes, including diet, lifestyle, and underlying medical conditions like gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD). To start, try avoiding trigger foods like spicy or acidic items, eating smaller meals, and not lying down right after eating. If these changes don't help, we can discuss further evaluation and potential medications.
